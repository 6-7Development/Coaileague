## 🔒 Customer Retention Strategies

1. **Annual Contracts** (10% discount)
   - Lock in customers
   - Predictable revenue
   - Reduce churn

2. **Usage-Based Scaling**
   - Automatic tier upgrades as they grow
   - No friction in expansion
   - Revenue grows with customer success

3. **Network Effects**
   - Their employees depend on the portal
   - Their clients love the transparency
   - Switching costs become prohibitive

4. **Data Lock-In**
   - Years of historical data
   - Custom workflows configured
   - Integrations built
   - Hard to migrate away

## 📈 Key Metrics to Track

1. **GMV** (Gross Merchandise Volume): Total $ processed
2. **Take Rate**: Avg platform fee % across all customers
3. **MRR**: Monthly Recurring Revenue (subscriptions)
4. **Transaction Revenue**: Monthly platform fees
5. **Add-on Attach Rate**: % of customers buying add-ons
6. **ARPA** (Average Revenue Per Account)
7. **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Customer lifetime value vs acquisition cost
8. **Net Revenue Retention**: >110% target (upsells > churn)
9. **Gross Margin**: Target 85-90%
