How to preserve 99% automation in practice

mappings are verified (customer_qbo_id, item_qbo_id, etc.)

no anomalies (hours spike, missing shift confirmations, rate mismatch, etc.)

Require approval only if a “risk signal” fires:

ambiguous client/employee match

new customer would be created

invoice total deviates from baseline threshold

missing service item/class/location mapping

That’s how you stay at 99% automation and still avoid disasters.

C. Your state machine needs to support “auto-send unless blocked”
