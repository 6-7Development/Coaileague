**CHATROOMS PAGE - SECURITY-SPECIFIC DESIGN:**

**Purpose:**
Guards are isolated at different sites (mall, warehouse, office building). They need to:
- Communicate with dispatch/management
- Coordinate with other guards
- Report incidents in real-time
- Get site-specific instructions
- Emergency communication

**Chatroom Types:**

1. **Site-Specific Chatrooms:**
   - One chatroom per client site
   - Only guards assigned to that site + management
   - Example: "Westfield Mall - Night Shift"
   - Persistent (doesn't close when shift ends)

2. **Shift-Based Chatrooms:**
   - One chatroom per active shift
   - Auto-created when shift starts
   - Auto-archived when shift ends
   - Example: "Tuesday Night Crew - 10pm-6am"

3. **Emergency/Incident Chatrooms:**
   - Created on-demand for incidents
   - Escalates to management immediately
   - Tagged with severity (low/medium/high/critical)

4. **Company-Wide Announcements:**
   - Management → All guards
   - Read-only for guards
   - For policy updates, schedule changes, etc.
