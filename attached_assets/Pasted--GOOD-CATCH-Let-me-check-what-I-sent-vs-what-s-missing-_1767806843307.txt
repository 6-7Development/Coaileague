2. TIMESHEET APPROVAL WORKFLOW

Manager approves/rejects time entries:

PENDING TIMESHEETS VIEW:
- Filter: Show only "Pending Approval"
- Sort: By employee, by date, by total hours
- Highlight discrepancies (late, GPS fail, etc.)

APPROVE FLOW:
1. Manager reviews time entries
2. Clicks [Approve] on employee row
3. Confirmation: "Approve 41.5 hours for John Smith?"
4. Status changes to ✅ Approved
5. Employee notified (optional)
6. Hours locked (cannot be edited without un-approving)

EDIT FLOW (If adjustment needed):
1. Click [Edit] on time entry
2. Modal opens with editable fields:
   - Clock in time
   - Clock out time
   - Break duration
   - Notes (required for edits: "Why are you adjusting?")
3. [Save Changes]
4. Audit log created: "Manager edited clock in from 7:02 PM to 7:00 PM - Reason: Employee forgot to clock in, confirmed via text"
5. Employee notified of adjustment

FLAG ISSUE FLOW:
1. Click [⚠️ Flag Issue]
2. Select reason:
   - Late clock in
   - Early clock out
   - GPS verification failed
   - Unauthorized overtime
   - Missing break
   - Other (specify)
3. Add notes
4. [Submit Flag]
5. Employee notified (must respond/explain)
6. Manager reviews response before approving
